Franklin City News

The fourth annual Kids Arts Festival of Tennessee is scheduled for this Saturday, June 2nd, at Pinkerton Park. The Festival, a joint venture between the City of Franklin Parks Department and the Williamson County Cultural Arts Commission is from 10 a.m. until 3 p.m. and is free and open to the public.
