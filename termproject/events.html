Cycle the Continental Divide

Are you looking for an unforgettable adventure overcoming boundaries and challenges?
Our annual global event brings together the most diverse cyclists in the world to join together to traverse paths and directions around the globe in a unique and grand expedition! Be sure that this will be the greatest and most beautiful experience on two wheels that you can experience, knowing new places and making new friendships of the most diverse cultures.

Frequency: September 15, 2018

Trail Length: 620 miles in 20 days

Skill Level Required: Advanced / Professional

Location: To be defined for this year

Cost: $1000 / cyclist (+ personal expenses)
