Bike Shop

Bozeman Local Tour

If you are looking for adventures in a city full of surprises, you will be amazed at what Bozeman can offer you. In this day trail you will know some of the most beautiful landscapes of the region and recharge your batteries receiving everything that nature has to offer you

Frequency: Daily with tour guide

Trail Length: 1,684 miles

Skill Level Required: Beginner

Location: Starting in front of our Store

Cost: $25 / cyclist

If you have more questions, feel free to contact us!

A Night to Remember

If you are looking for an adventure unlike anything you've ever done, with direct contact with nature and a unique view of the grandeur and beauty of the starry night sky, this is the right tour! We offer all the security and experience you need for your safety, making sure you have an unforgettable night!

Frequency: Daily with tour guide

Trail Length: 2,684 miles

Skill Level Required: Intermediate to Advanced

Location: Starting on the determined point of contact with the foot of the mountain

Cost: $50 / cyclist

If you have more questions, feel free to contact us!
